Internships

NeuroLeap summer '23

During my internship with NeuroLeap, a company that uses neuroscience technology to improve brain function, I gained valuable experience in web development and database management.

As an intern, I was responsible for building and maintaining the company's website using HTML and CSS, ensuring that it was user-friendly and visually appealing. Additionally, I used NodeJS to create interactive features such as contact forms and chatbots to improve user engagement.

I also worked on implementing a database system using SQL to store and manage patient information, which was a critical component of the company's operations. This involved creating tables, designing queries, and ensuring the integrity and security of the data.

What I took away

In addition to technical skills, the internship also taught me important soft skills such as time management, problem-solving, and adaptability. These skills have proven to be essential in my academic and professional pursuits, and have helped me to become a more well-rounded individual.

Overall, my web development internship was an incredible opportunity that allowed me to gain practical experience and to learn from experienced professionals in the industry. It has given me a competitive edge in the job market and has helped me to become a more confident and capable developer.
